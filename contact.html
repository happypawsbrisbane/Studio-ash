Questions

Frequently asked

How long does a project take?

Brand identity projects typically take 3-4 weeks. Website design is 4-6 weeks. Full brand + website packages run 6-8 weeks. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

What's included in the process?

Every project includes a discovery call, strategy phase, design concepts, revision rounds, and final file delivery. You'll be involved at every step.

Do you work with clients outside Brisbane?

Absolutely! I work with clients all over Australia and internationally. Everything can be done remotely via video calls and email.

What if I'm not sure what I need?

That's completely fine. Send me a message with a bit about your business and goals, and I'll help you figure out the best path forward. No pressure.
